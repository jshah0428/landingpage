Projects

Stock Market Analyzer

This project provides real-time and historical stock prices, ensuring users have the most up-to-date information. Each user is connected to a unique profile, which allows them to buy and sell stocks, view their stock holdings, and deposit money seamlessly. Additionally, the platform delivers news relevant to users' stock holdings, empowering them to make informed stock actions.

Outdoor Clothing and Company

This project features a fictitious outdoor clothing store where users can browse a diverse selection of items. Managers have the ability to effortlessly add or delete items, handle shipments, and manage inventory.
